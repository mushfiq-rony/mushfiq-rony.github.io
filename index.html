Background

About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Data Science at the University of Mississippi Medical Center (2024–present). My research interests span biostatistics, machine learning in healthcare, and functional data analysis.

I hold a BS in Statistics (CGPA 3.81/4.0) from the University of Dhaka, where I developed a foundation in statistical inference, multivariate analysis, and clinical data management.

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a Data Management Assistant at icddr,b — one of the world's leading diarrheal disease research centers — and as a Research Assistant at Save the Children Bangladesh, contributing to maternal and newborn health analytics.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning for clinical prediction & disease diagnosis
  • Functional & longitudinal data analysis
  • Biostatistics for randomized controlled trials
  • Neurocognitive analytics & sleep science
  • Diarrheal disease epidemiology & surveillance
  • Maternal and child health indicators
  • Statistical computing in R and Python
