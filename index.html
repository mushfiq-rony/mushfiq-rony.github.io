Background

About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in Data Science at the University of Mississippi Medical Center (2024–present). My research is currently focused on functional data analysis, with an emphasis on functional regression methods applied to complex biomedical signals and neurocognitive data.

Alongside my PhD, I work part-time as a Student Biostatistician at the MIND Center, UMMC, where I contribute to research projects at the intersection of neuroscience, sleep science, and statistical modeling.

I hold a BS in Statistics from the University of Dhaka, and have prior professional experience at icddr,b and Save the Children Bangladesh, working on clinical trials and maternal health analytics.

Research Interests

  • Functional data analysis & functional regression
  • Variable domain functional regression
  • Neurocognitive analytics & sleep science (OSA)
  • Machine learning for clinical prediction
  • Biostatistics for randomized controlled trials
  • Diarrheal disease epidemiology & surveillance
  • Maternal and child health indicators
